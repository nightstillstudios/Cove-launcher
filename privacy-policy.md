## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Night Still Studios ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and handles information in connection with Cove Launcher (the "App"), an Android home screen launcher application. By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information Collected Automatically

**Crash Reports and Performance Data**

If you enable Usage Reports in General Settings, we collect anonymous crash reports and performance data through Google Firebase Crashlytics and Firebase Analytics. This data includes:

- Device model and manufacturer
- Android version
- App version
- Crash stack traces and error logs
- App performance metrics

Usage Reports are **disabled by default**. You may enable or disable this feature at any time in General Settings. When disabled, no analytics or crash data is collected or transmitted.

### 2.2 Information You Provide or Authorise

**Location Data**

If you enable the Weather Widget, the App requests access to your device's location to retrieve local weather conditions. Location data is used solely for the purpose of fetching weather information and is not stored by us, not logged, and not transmitted to us or any third party beyond the weather data request. You may revoke location permission at any time through Android Settings.

**Contacts**

If you enable Contact Search within the App's search settings, the App reads your device contacts to display matching results when you perform a search. Contact data is processed entirely on your device and is never transmitted to us or any third party. You may revoke contacts permission at any time through Android Settings.

**Notifications**

If you enable Notification Dots or the Music Widget, the App uses Android's Notification Listener Service to read notification metadata for the purpose of displaying notification badges on app icons and media information in the music widget. Notification content is processed locally on your device and is never stored, transmitted, or shared. You may disable this permission at any time through Android Settings.

---

## 3. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit the following, regardless of your settings:

- Your installed application list. Although the App requires access to all installed packages to function as a home screen launcher, this information remains on your device and is never transmitted to us or any third party.
- App usage patterns used for sort-by-usage functionality. These are stored entirely on your device.
- App category data. Categories are determined using a lookup file bundled within the App and on-device processing only.
- Any personally identifiable information beyond what is explicitly described in this policy.

---

## 4. On-Device Processing

The following features operate entirely on your device. No data from these features is transmitted externally:

- **App drawer organisation and categories** — determined using a locally bundled category reference file
- **Sort by usage** — usage patterns stored and processed locally
- **Depth Effect** — foreground and background subject segmentation is performed using Google ML Kit running on your device; no images or wallpaper data are transmitted externally
- **Hidden apps** — stored locally in encrypted app preferences
- **All launcher settings and preferences** — stored locally using Android DataStore

---

## 5. Third-Party Services

### 5.1 Google Firebase

When Usage Reports are enabled, crash and performance data is transmitted to Google Firebase. This data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy, available at [https://policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy). We do not sell or share Firebase data with any other third party.

### 5.2 Weather Data Provider

When the Weather Widget is enabled, your device location is transmitted to a weather data provider to retrieve current weather conditions. No personally identifying information accompanies this request. The weather provider's privacy practices are governed by their own privacy policy.

### 5.3 Google ML Kit

The Depth Effect feature uses Google ML Kit's Subject Segmentation API. ML Kit processing occurs on-device. No images, wallpaper data, or processing results are transmitted to Google or any other party. ML Kit's terms are available at [https://developers.google.com/ml-kit/terms](https://developers.google.com/ml-kit/terms).

---

## 6. Permissions

The App requests the following Android permissions. Each permission is used solely as described:

| Permission | Purpose | Required |
|---|---|---|
| `QUERY_ALL_PACKAGES` | Display all installed apps in the app drawer and enable app launching | Yes — core launcher functionality |
| `VIBRATE` | Provide haptic feedback for interactions | Yes — core launcher functionality |
| `SET_ALARM` | Enable alarm shortcuts in search results | Yes — core launcher functionality |
| `ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION` | Retrieve local weather for the Weather Widget | No — optional, user-enabled |
| `READ_CONTACTS` | Display contact results in search | No — optional, user-enabled |
| `BIND_NOTIFICATION_LISTENER_SERVICE` | Display notification dots and media information | No — optional, user-enabled |

## 7. Data Retention

We do not retain personally identifiable information. Anonymous crash and performance data transmitted through Firebase is retained in accordance with Google Firebase's data retention policies.

All locally stored data — including settings, preferences, wallpaper selections, and hidden app lists — remains on your device and is deleted when you clear the App's data or uninstall the App.

---

## 8. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical measures to protect the information we handle. Locally stored data is stored using Android's DataStore with application-level isolation. Optional usage report data transmitted to Firebase is encrypted in transit.

No method of transmission or storage is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of any data.

## 10. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights and controls:

- **Disable crash reporting and analytics** — turn off Usage Reports in General Settings at any time
- **Revoke permissions** — revoke location, contacts, or notification access at any time through Android Settings

- **Uninstall** — uninstalling the App removes all locally stored data from your device
